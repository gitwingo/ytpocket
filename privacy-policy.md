# YTpocket Helper — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 26, 2026**

## Overview
YTpocket Helper is a browser extension that works alongside the YTpocket 
desktop application to enable seamless YouTube downloading.

## Data We Collect
The extension collects YouTube and Google authentication cookies from your 
browser. These cookies are used solely to authenticate download requests 
made by the YTpocket desktop app.

## How Your Data Is Used
- Cookies are read from your browser and passed directly to the YTpocket 
  desktop app installed on your PC via the browser's native messaging API.
- Cookies are written to a local file on your PC only 
  (`%APPDATA%\YTpocket\cookies.txt`).
- Your data is **never transmitted to any remote server**.
- Your data is **never sold, shared, or used for any purpose** other than 
  authenticating download requests within YTpocket.

## Data Storage
- A timestamp and cookie count are stored locally in the extension's browser 
  storage to display the last sync time in the popup.
- No personal data is stored in the cloud or on any external server.

## Third Parties
We do not share any data with third parties. Ever.
